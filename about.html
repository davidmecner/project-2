Biography

David Milan is a 3D artist and designer, working across multiple disciplines including typography and illustration. He sees letters from another perspective, and likes to get away from the idea of “perfection that surrounds lettering and typography nowadays. He loves working and experimenting with colors, textures, shapes, and forms.

Clients

His clients include: Facebook, Adobe, Pepsi, Huawei, Procreate, Desigual, Washington Post, NBC, Wacom, Crocs, MTV, and Wix.

Tips for new artists

Some of the David’s tips is to think about the strokes since they are a very important element in order to create a nice lettering piece, also the thickness and kerning are very important as well. Sometimes he has the same exact mistakes, David usually sees different works of great artists to have a better idea of how to do it and that’s how David improves his work!
